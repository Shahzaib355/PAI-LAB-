About Hadith Verification

All hadiths displayed are from authentic collections including:

  • Sahih al-Bukhari - Compiled by Imam Muhammad al-Bukhari (d. 870 CE)
  • Sahih Muslim - Compiled by Imam Muslim ibn al-Hajjaj (d. 875 CE)
  • Sunan al-Tirmidhi - Compiled by Imam al-Tirmidhi (d. 892 CE)
  • Sunan Abu Dawud - Compiled by Imam Abu Dawud (d. 889 CE)
  • Sunan Ibn Majah - Compiled by Imam Ibn Majah (d. 887 CE)

These collections are part of the Kutub al-Sittah (The Six Books), which are the most trusted hadith collections in Sunni Islam.
